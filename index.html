OBJECTIVE

The objective of this case study was to provide homeowners an easier way stay on top of home maintenance.

GOAL

Design an uncomplicated app that will assist homeowners of all skill levels to keep their home maintained and track the types of items that need replacement.

MY ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Research and Analysis
  • Wireframe
  • Lofi Prototype

TIMELINE

Over a three week period, our team researched, developed a possible solution, performed user testing, and developed a prototype.

THE PROBLEM

Homeowners find it difficult to organize and remember maintenance intervals that come along with owning a home. They need a way to efficiently and swiftly be able to keep track of everything that needs to be maintained, cleaned, and replaced before more costly damages occur.

THE PROCESS

Through user research, ideation, and prototyping, we were able to find a resolution to this issue. Much of the user research was conducted as a group, along with brainstorming sessions to move us towards a possible solution. The people we targeted for our research were homeowners, or renters, that were responsible for the upkeep and maintenance of their property.

    The 6 interviews found:

  • Homeowner had problems keeping track of what types of lightbulbs were needed for various fixtures.
  • Issues with knowing the size and type of air and water filters they need, and when they should be replaced.
  • Seasonal chores such as gutter cleaning and landscape maintenance were often forgotten.  
  • Those interviewed did express a desire for a tool to help them with these issues.

USER FLOW

Used user flow to map out how the app would function, and paper wireframes were developed based on the flow. This demonstrates how links off site and to pdfs can be removed to improve the feel of the site.

FIRST LOFI PROTOTYPE

The design allows users to save items by category and room. This allows homeowners to quickly reference the specifications for different relplacement items. By allowing the phone to get the product information using a barcode scan, users can quickly input items.

USER TESTING PLAN

Task 1

Add an item to the inventory using the scan barcode feature.

Task 2

Create a new reminder to update the calendar.

Task 3

Return to the home page and then view an item in the inveotry list.
